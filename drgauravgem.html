Patient Awareness

Uterine Fibroids Treatment Without Hysterectomy

Uterine fibroids are non-cancerous benign growths in the uterus causing severe bleeding or pain. Instead of removing the organ via open surgery, look at the target indicators below:

Heavy Menstrual Flow

Prolonged cycles causing continuous lethargy or low iron.

Pelvic Pain & Pressure

Constant fullness in lower abdominal regions causing distress.

Uterine Artery Embolization (UAE)

Through a single micro-catheter entry point, we block the tiny arterial branches delivering blood straight to the fibroids. Deprived of oxygenated flow, the fibroids shrink and resolve seamlessly over time.
